Top 3 Things I Need for My Hobby

  • Badminton Racket
  • Shuttlecock
  • Large Space or a Badminton Court

Steps to Get Started

  1. Before playing, make sure to get some warm up to prevent having cramps or worse an injury.
  2. After that, start with a relax play by playing a lob shots with the racket and shuttle.
  3. When you are ready, start your play with a serve of either forehand or backhand serve.
  4. Lastly, never forget to enjoy playing badminton especially with other people!

    Enjoying what you are doing is best in learning to get better! :)
